Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 15, 2024

Bonnie A Ross Coaching, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our coaching services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Birth information (date, time, and location) for Quantum Human Design Reports
  • Information shared during coaching sessions
  • Communication preferences and feedback

Automatically Collected Information: We may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including IP address, browser type, and access times.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide coaching services and support
  • Generate Quantum Human Design Reports using your birth information
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Communicate with you about services and appointments
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers (such as Stripe for payment processing and Bodygraph.com for generating Human Design Reports) who assist us in operating our business
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or transfer of our business

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: For payment processing. Stripe has its own privacy policy governing the collection and use of payment information. Review Stripe's privacy policy at https://stripe.com/privacy.
  • Bodygraph.com: For generating Quantum Human Design Reports using your birth information. Your birth data is shared with Bodygraph.com solely for report generation purposes. Review Bodygraph.com's privacy policy at their website.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by law. Coaching session notes and communications may be retained for professional and legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your personal information

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website with an updated effective date.
